Hannah Chen

I am at my happiest with a camera in one hand and chopsticks in the other.

I am Hannah Chen, a dual master’s student at the University of Michigan, pursuing an MBA at the Stephen M. Ross School of Business and a Master’s at the School of Information. Before coming to the U.S. for graduate studies, I worked in the banking industry and customized financial solutions for corporate clients in diverse industries.

a Relentless Design + Business Integrator

I found myself passionate about the combination of business and design since I was at the age of 7. At that time, I design a series of perfume bottles for my mom as her birthday gift. And the funny thing was that I price-tagged all of them! I guess I had no intention to charge my mom for her birthday gift…but somehow the business idea also came into play at that time.

Passion for Experiential Living and Social Impact

In the summer of 2017, I signed up my very first Airbnb Experience Tour -Wolf Encounter in Seattle. I still remember the host, Mr. Dave Coleburn, shared with me that Airbnb has made the lives of animals in his refuge better. Because of Airbnb platform, Dave can run the experience tour and use the proceeds to raise and conserve animals in his animal refuge. Regarding the Experience itself, I’d never imagined one day I can walk with a WOLF, yes a real one. And I never imaged the WOLF would LEAK my face.
