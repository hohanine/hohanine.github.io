Project Food Deco

I am at my happiest with a camera in one hand and chopsticks in the other.

Food is my life inspiration. I have traveled to over 15 countries.
Everytime, I research heavily on the unique local food experience and invited my friends and family to taste it together. Of course, not every food experience is perfect. However, I choose to stay open-minded and appreciate the food I meet.
And food is not just about its taste but also its decoration. The best food decoration catches the best balance between simplicity and complexity. It is an art.
I especially enjoy the time when I am able to have a dialogue with the chef or restaurant manager. From them, you learn their personal stories, their loves to the restaurants, and their passion and creativity to the food.
